What is Scriptures NFT?

The Bible is one of the most powerful and influential pieces of literature on the planet. Its ability to stay relevant throughout the ages and create joy in others makes it the perfect book to make into an NFT. The team at Scriptures NFT understands the importance of this book and is working hard to give this project the respect it deserves. We detest the commodification of holy scripture and for that reason will donate all profits made from this project to non-profit organizations chosen by the ScripturesNFT community!

Our Story

Scriptures NFT was co-founded by a group of like-minded, Crypto-Christians. As we watched Defi revolutionize the Finance industry we saw an opportunity to leverage Blockchain technology and finally bring Christianity into the 21st Century. ScripturesNFT is much more than your average jpeg/pfp NFT project. We want to decentralize the Church by creating a community of Christians who are willing to help and support one another while spreading the Word of God. Through the combination of holy scripture and the blockchain, we no longer need to rely on a centralized organization like the Church. Each of us has the power to find God through our own interpretation of the holy scriptures and unlike a physical church, we can bring Christians together on a global scale. FAQs

ScripturesNFT is a collection of over 30k NFTs representing each verse in the KJV Bible.

Our mission is to foster a global community of Crypto-Christians who share a common goal of wanting to become closer with God and others through prayer, scripture, and community discussions and events.

Profits will be put towards future ScripturesNFT sponsored projects and non-profit donations chosen by the community.

ScripturesNFT will hold community events open to everyone such as prayer circles, bible readings, and open discussions about scripture and current events. ScripturesNFT Holders will be granted access to take part in special prize events, the first being our Art Raffle. In the future, we hope to hold annual in-person events with all of our wonderful community members!

Each ScripturesNFT you purchase will represent one raffle ticket. Each raffle ticket gives you the chance to win a Christian-inspired NFT created by a professional artist. Details surrounding the Artists and Prize-NFTs will be revealed in the coming weeks.

The theme for ScripturesNFT is a calculated metaphor for the core vision of this project. We are here to bring Christianity into the 21st century and appeal to a new generation of Christians. However, make no mistake, although we are very different from a traditional church, we believe the word of God should always remain in its purest form.
